First Post!

21/04/2018

So here it is: I've finally taken the time to write up some basic code. After completing the beginners' course through Code First: Girls not too long ago, I was itching for a chance to put the skills I learned into use and push my web-design skills further; however, I realised that I would have to make such an opportunity for myself.

I may not have a lot of time in the coming weeks to make this blog particularly beautiful, but I hope to work on this in the long term, adding features to test out skills and also commenting on my day-to-day life as a university student.

This next year marks the cutting of my 'safety net' that education has afforded me these past years. After I graduate, I'll (hopefully) find a position where I will be forced to adapt to a new working environment: I hope that I find something that challenges me, educates me, and ultimately gives me a feeling of accomplishment at the end of the day.
